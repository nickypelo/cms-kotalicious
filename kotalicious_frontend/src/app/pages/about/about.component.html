Inspiration

UCT, an institution with a diverse group of people, has many people who have many tastes in food and what they consider fast food. Upon realisation, we noticed many did not even know what a Kota was.

From international students and even local students from Western Cape, Northern Cape, Eastern Cape, KwaZulu-Natal, Mpumalanga. Kota - this fast food sandwich, from Gauteng is a quick meal that fills you up at an affordable price. I put it upon myself to share it and make it better that even those that know about it are blown away.
